Why compliance matters

AHPRA compliance
isn't a checkbox.
It's the foundation.

Healthcare advertising in Australia is regulated by AHPRA. Patient testimonials, before-and-after comparisons, and outcome guarantees are restricted or prohibited across most disciplines. Generic agencies breach these rules regularly — sometimes without knowing it.

Every piece of content and every campaign Qurated produces for healthcare clients is built with compliance as the baseline, not an afterthought. Your marketing grows your practice. It doesn't put your registration at risk.
