What actually happens

In plain terms.
No agency speak.

You've probably heard the pitch before. So here's what we actually do — for a trade business, in the language of someone who understands you're on the tools all day, not behind a desk.

01
Your work. On camera. Posted.

We come to you. Shoot the jobs worth showing — before, during, after. You don't touch a camera, write a caption, or think about posting. We handle all of it every month. Your Instagram and Facebook start looking like the standard of work you actually deliver.

02
Ads that show up in your suburbs — not theirs.

We run Meta ads targeting homeowners in the specific suburbs you want to work in. Not broad. Not random. The right people see your work at the right time — and when they click, they go somewhere professional that closes the lead, not a Facebook page from 2019.

03
No enquiry goes unanswered — even at 7pm.

When a job enquiry comes in while you're on the roof, it doesn't sit unread until morning. The system responds immediately, qualifies what they need, and sets up the callback. You get back to it when you're ready. They don't go to the next bloke on the list.

04
Google finds you before your competitors.

Your Google Business Profile, local SEO, and review strategy all run in the background. When someone in your suburb types "electrician near me" or "best plumber Wangara" — you show up. Not some directory. Not the company from the other side of Perth. You.

05
You stop quoting jobs that go nowhere.

When people contact you after seeing your work and reading your reviews, they've already decided. Better enquiries. Better clients. Less time burning petrol on quotes that go to whoever was cheapest. The system qualifies them before they get to you.
